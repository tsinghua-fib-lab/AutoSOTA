## Method

The paper uses a Graph Moral Network (GMN) approach to propagate moral information through a word association graph. The key formula is:

```
information = (I - alpha * T)^{-1} * initial_values
```

where `T` is the symmetrically normalized adjacency matrix and `initial_values` are binary MFD (Moral Foundations Dictionary) scores.
